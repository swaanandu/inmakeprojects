About

The Best technical service provider, registered in the Emirate of Dubai.

Innovative Safe Technical Service Contracting LLC is a technical service provider, registered in the emirate of Dubai under Economic Development Authority to provide turnkey solutions for Residential, commercial and Industrial properties. The company is headed and managed by well experienced Technical & Management Professionals and are well versed with the changing trends and techniques of the Construction industry. IST is under the umbrella of its parent company Al Wasl which is established in 2004 providing various professional services in the field of Accounting, Auditing and Business advisory services through various process within an organization with more than 400 satisfied clients from different business spectrums.
